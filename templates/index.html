LastCall

When I lived in NYC, I was an avid fan of the food-saving app Too Good to Go.

It was founded in Denmark using a grassroots approach to collaborate with local restaurants and eateries, selling food that would have been discarded at a steep discount. I've trekked many miles in NYC using this app.

LastCall reimagines food-saving apps and streamlines the user experience for an emerging model that is still unfamiliar to many.
